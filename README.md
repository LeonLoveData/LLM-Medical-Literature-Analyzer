# LLM-Medical-Literature-Analyzer
Leverage AI and Literature For Insights

## Project Overview
Gene & RNA Therapeutics Intelligence using LLM(LangChain) and NLP.

## Dataset
Pubmed

## Questions to Be Answered
1. What are the emerging scientific and technological trends in gene and RNA therapeutics across literature and patents?

2. Which targets and diseases show the strongest evidence supporting gene or RNA therapeutic approaches?

3. What does the competitive landscape look like for specific modalities, delivery systems, targets, or disease areas?

4. Which companies are advancing the fastest in gene and RNA therapy pipelines, and where are the white‑space opportunities?

5. What are the key efficacy and safety findings reported across preclinical and clinical studies for a given modality or target?

6. What are the major risks, safety concerns, and technical limitations associated with different gene and RNA therapeutic platforms?

## Methodology
- Python
- GROQ API
- LangChain
- PubMed API
- NLP

## Key Insights
the competitive landscape is dynamic and rapidly evolving, with multiple competitors vying for market share in various therapeutic areas. The strategic threat level for each competitor is influenced by factors such as clinical positioning, patent positioning, and market competition.
